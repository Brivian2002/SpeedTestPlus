About Speed Test Plus

We believe everyone deserves to know the true speed of their internet connection — for free, with no strings attached.

Our Mission

Speed Test Plus was built with a simple goal: provide the most accurate, user-friendly, and privacy-respecting internet speed test on the web. In an age where internet speed affects everything from remote work to entertainment, knowing your true connection speed is essential.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Accuracy First

We use multi-connection testing with statistical analysis, testing against Cloudflare's global edge network for the most precise measurements possible.

Privacy by Design

Zero data collection, zero tracking, zero registration required. Your speed test results stay on your device — we never see them.

Free for Everyone

Speed Test Plus is and will always be free. Internet speed testing is a basic utility that shouldn't be behind a paywall.

Education

We don't just give you numbers — we help you understand what they mean and how to improve your connection with comprehensive guides.

Works Everywhere

Test from any device, any browser, any location. Our responsive design ensures a perfect experience on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Modern Technology

Built with modern web standards — fast loading, no bloat, no unnecessary dependencies. Speed Test Plus is as fast as the speeds it measures.

Why Choose Us

How We're Different

Unlike other speed test tools, Speed Test Plus was designed from the ground up with modern web principles:

  • No App Required: Works entirely in your browser — no downloads, no installations
  • No Registration: Click and test. That's it. No accounts, no email, no sign-ups
  • No Tracking: We don't use analytics trackers or tracking cookies
  • Cloudflare-Powered: We test against Cloudflare's edge network, with servers in 300+ cities for minimal geographic bias
  • ISP-Independent: Our results aren't influenced by ISP partnerships or "fast lanes"
  • Educational: Comprehensive guides help you understand results and take action
  • Test History: Track your speeds over time with local storage — only on your device
